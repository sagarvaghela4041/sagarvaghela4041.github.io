Projects

Wizcart - A Service Marketplace

Wizcart is an e-commerce platform in which various service providers and customers can set up their profiles and sell or buy various kinds of services. Customers can check different services by categories and sub-categories and place orders by selecting the required date and time for delivery and desired provider from the given matching providers' list using the customer portal. All services and users can be maintained by wizcart admin from the role-based admin portal. Providers are also further categorized by roles. Admin can add services and categories with multiple available date ranges, geographical locations, details, and configurations like packages, questions and answers, and mapping of them with the package. Users can track the order and see the order history. Both providers and customers can modify, or reschedule orders at a predefined time in the order life cycle. Other functionalities include feedback, disputes, marketing, etc.

Notes MarketPlace

Notes MarketPlace is an online marketplace for students to buy and sell their notes. Notes can be either paid or free. Users can directly search for the notes by search feature with filters including university, category, course information, etc. Users can surf the site anonymously and can download the free notes available, if the user wants to download a paid note then the user has to register or log in to the portal. Users can see their downloaded and uploaded notes and status in the list. Users can give ratings to a particular note after downloading it, the average rating is shown on the notes details page along with other details. Uploaded notes of users will be verified by admins and then published. Admins can log in through the same portal by using credentials given by the super admin. admins can see all activities of users using various filters and dashboards. Different emails are sent to respective users on various events.

BounceBack

BounceBack is an app for anyone who has an orthopedic injury and wants to gain an edge in their recovery. After registration users are asked to give answers to some pre-defined questions. Based on the answers given by the users, potential user matches are listed, and users can send them a buddy request and connect with them. Users can write a post about their experience and add reactions, comments, reply to the comment to the post of other users. Users can block or report the user, post, or comment. users can update their recovery journey step by step. Using the chat feature users can communicate with each other after becoming buddies. Admin can get the list of all registered users with their details. Admin can see how many users have blocked or reported a particular user, post, or comment through the admin panel. BounceBack is available for both Android and iOS devices.
